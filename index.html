How it works

Three steps.
Zero confusion.

No manuals. No lookup tables. Just point, scan, and toss with confidence.

1
🌍
Choose your country

Select where you are. recAIcle loads the exact waste regulations for your location — down to the city level for premium users.

2
📸
Snap a photo

Open the in-app camera and point it at whatever you're not sure about. Our AI identifies the item in under a second.

3
✅
Toss it right

Get a clear, localized answer: which bin, how to prepare it, and why. No guessing, no fines, no contaminated recycling batches.

Features

Built for real life,
not just demos.

🌐
Multi-country regulations

Japan, Germany, Taiwan, South Korea — every country has different rules. We've built a continuously updated database so you don't have to.

🤖
AI image recognition

Our model is trained specifically on waste items — not just generic objects. It handles crumpled, dirty, and unlabelled items.

🧩
Composite material guide

Premium

For people who
actually care.

Premium isn't about removing ads. It's about features that genuinely change how you sort waste every day.

🗂️
Scan history & eco footprint

Every item you scan is logged. See monthly stats — kg of waste sorted, CO₂ saved, and a breakdown by category. Make your impact visible.

🧩
Composite material breakdown

AI identifies each layer of complex items — a foil-lined paper cup, a labelled glass bottle — and gives you step-by-step disposal instructions.

✈️
Offline mode

Download full ruleset for any country before you travel. No WiFi needed — ideal for Japan, Germany, or any country with strict sorting rules.

📍
City-level precision

Rules in Tokyo differ from Osaka. Premium users get district-level accuracy, not just national defaults. The most precise sorting guide available.

⚡
Home screen widget

One tap from your home screen launches the scanner instantly. Because when you're standing at a bin with full hands, every second counts.

🏢
B2B ESG dashboard

For companies. Deploy to your team, track collective waste compliance, and export reports for ESG disclosures — all from one dashboard.
